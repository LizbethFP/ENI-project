Negocios Nacionales is a Peruvian company created in 2016, dedicated to the fishery products production and export, and managed by professionals with more than 20 years in the fishing industry. Currently, the company is versed to fishery production and broking in order to cover all orders, making sure to always have stock. The corporation relies on specialized technical personnel and processes in cold storage plants that have sanitary permits to export to different markets such as Asia, Europe and the United States of America, complying with current sanitary regulations.

Mission

Provide excellent customer service with high quality products processed according to international quality standards.

Vision

Aim to reach the world market with high quality products and very competitive prices, creating new jobs and new assets to the country.

Values

  1. Honesty: Act with transparency and ethics as a company and as individuals.
  2. Teamwork: Collaborators trust built on good communication and integration so as to achieve work goals.
  3. Responsibility: Commitment to act consistently and accept consequences of actions taken as a collaborator.
  4. Integrity: Integrity and loyalty when making decisions, taking into account the company’s ethics and best interest.
  5. Respect: Value people’s different customs and cultures, without discrimination.
